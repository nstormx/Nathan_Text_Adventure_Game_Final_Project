The heroes journey begins at the Kingdom of Eqinox, where the king has sent word to his people about a mysterious dungeon that has been named "The Grand Zyelow Dungeon", and it's been located to the north of the Paragon Forest. The king warns adventures and travelers to be wary of the place and the nearby woods, as monsters sightings are said to have been reported their. You have caught wind of this and proceed to the dungeon to not only clear out the bad guys inside and stop them from endangering anyone but also to possibly find some treasure. You make your way through the forest and find yourself at the entrance of the dungeon...
